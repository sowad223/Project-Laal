Our Projects

Project Flood Relief Comilla

Location: Comilla, Bangladesh
Objective: To provide immediate assistance to communities affected by flooding.
Impact: This project aims to supply clean drinking water, food, and temporary shelter to those displaced by floods. We also focus on long-term recovery efforts, including rebuilding homes and infrastructure.

Project Clean Dhaka

Location: Dhaka, Bangladesh
Objective: To clean and restore public spaces affected by waste and pollution.
Impact: This initiative involves organizing community clean-up drives, educating residents about waste management, and establishing recycling programs to promote a cleaner environment.

Project Sustainable Livelihoods

Location: Dhaka, Bangladesh
Objective: To empower flood-affected families through sustainable livelihood options.
Impact: This project aims to provide vocational training and resources to help families generate income, reducing their dependency on aid and fostering resilience against future disasters.
